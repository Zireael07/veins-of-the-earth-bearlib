Default keys:
Arrows to move
Home to move up-left, PageUp to move up-right
PageDown to move left-down, End to move right-down
L to open log

Vi keys:
h to move up, j to move down, k to move up, l to move right
y to move up-left, u to move up-right
b to move left-down, n to move left-right
Shift+M to open log

> to ascend/descend stairs
G to pick up items, D to drop items, I to open inventory
C to open character sheet
? to bring up this menu again
